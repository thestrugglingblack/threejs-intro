🤓 Fun Facts About Gingers and Maine Coons! 🤓

Gingers

  • Gingers are predominately males due to the ginger gene being carried in the X chromosome. The ratio is 80% for a male ginger and 20% for a female ginger.
  • Legend says they have the "M" mark on their head because they encountered "Jesus"
  • Most gingers have four distinct patterns in their coat: spotted, mackerel, stripped and ticked.

Maine Coons

  • Maine coon is the oldest cat breed to native America.
  • They have muscular builds and often mistaken for a bobcat. They can weight between 8-18lbs.
  • They are basically dogs of the cat world....very vocal and will follow you around
  • Maine coons are built for harsh weather due to their water-resistant fur and snowshoe paws.
  • Maine coon carry the gene for the extra toe which is basically a polydactyl cat.
  • A Main coon was featured in one of the Harry Potter movies!
